Frequently Asked Questions

How does the supply chain system work?
Our supply chain system connects farmers directly with buyers, ensuring transparency, fair pricing, and efficiency in transactions.
How does this platform benefit farmers?
Farmers can sell their products at better prices, get access to a wider customer base, and reduce dependency on middlemen, ensuring fair profits.
How does this platform benefit consumers?
Consumers get access to fresh farm produce at affordable prices directly from farmers, ensuring quality and fair pricing.
Is this website is multilingual?
Yes, You can use this website in your desired language.
Do I need to create an account to use this platform?
Yes, you need to sign up as a farmer or consumer to place orders, list products, or access full features of the platform.
Is there a registration fee for farmers or consumers?
No, registration is completely free for both farmers and consumers.
How do I register as a farmer or consumer?
Click on the Sign Up button and select whether you are a farmer or a consumer. Fill in the required details to create your account. .
What types of products can be sold on this platform?
Farmers can list vegetables, fruits, grains, dairy products, and other farm-related goods following platform guidelines.
How is quality assurance handled?
All products go through strict quality checks before delivery to ensure high standards.
